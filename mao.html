HOW TO PLAY

                - Use the button with your name to open and close
                  your hand
                - Click on a card to select it
                - Make statements by clicking on them at the top
                        - Select 'Have a Nice Day' multiple
                          times to add 'Very' to the statement
                - Click 'Play Turn' to play your card
                - Skip your turn with your 'Pass Turn' button
